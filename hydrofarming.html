The Growth Cycle

Robotic precision from seed to harvest.

01

Smart Germination

Seeds are placed in non-soil substrates inside our climate-controlled germination chambers. Sensors monitor humidity and temperature to the micro-degree, ensuring 99.8% germination rates.

02

Aeroponic Misting

Instead of soaking roots, we spray them with a nutrient-rich mist every few minutes. This maximizes oxygen exposure to the root zone, accelerating growth rates by up to 3x compared to traditional soil.

03

Spectral Recipe

Our AI-driven LEDs adjust their light spectrum throughout the day. Blue light promotes vegetative growth, while red light enhances flowering (if needed) and leaf density. Every photon is calculated.

Technical Deep Dive: Precision Growth Science

Nutrient Stewardship

Soil is unpredictable. Our targeted mineral solutions deliver exact ratios of Macronutrients (N-P-K) and Micronutrients (Iron, Zinc, Manganese). Electrical Conductivity (EC) sensors monitor salt concentration every millisecond, ensuring roots never suffer osmotic stress or nutrient burn.

Vapor Pressure Deficit (VPD)

We don't just control temperature; we control plant transpiration. By managing VPD—the difference between the amount of moisture in the air vs. how much it can hold—we force plants to "drink" more or less aggressively. Optimal VPD (0.8 - 1.2 kPa) ensures maximum nutrient uptake without wilting.

Daily Light Integral (DLI)

Plants count photons. DLI measures the total amount of photosynthetically active radiation (PAR) received in a day. We tailor this for every crop—Lettuce gets 14 mol/m²/day, while Basil might get 12. This prevents energy waste and ensures uniform crop morphology.

Aeroponic Mechanics

High-pressure mist nozzles (50-80 microns) atomize the nutrient solution. This creates a "fog" that penetrates dense root structures deeper than liquid water can, allowing for 100% root oxygenation. The result is root hair development 300% denser than soil-growth.
